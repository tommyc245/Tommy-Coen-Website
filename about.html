About

Headshot

I'm a freshman studying Mechanical Engineering at Duke University. I grew up in Pacific Grove, California, where I spent four years on FRC Team 5104 — the BreakerBots — building competition robots and developing everything from custom gearboxes to autonomous vision systems.

I completed the CTE Engineering Pathway in high school, working through the Titans of CNC machining program and gaining hands-on experience with manual and CNC mills, lathes, and full CAD/CAM workflows.

At Duke, I'm a member of the Aero Club and continue building software tools on the side. I'm interested in problems that combine mechanical design, controls, and software — especially in robotics and aerospace.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy mountain biking, hiking, and skiing.
