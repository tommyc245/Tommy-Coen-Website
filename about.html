About

Headshot

I'm a freshman studying Mechanical Engineering at Duke University. I grew up in Pacific Grove, California, and spent four years on FRC Team 5104 — the BreakerBots — working my way from machining parts on the shop floor to Co-Captain and Design Lead.

Over four seasons I took on progressively more responsibility: starting as a machinist on Charybdis, becoming Manufacturing Lead and Drive Coach for the 2023 season, leading the mechanical design of Plop, then taking on Co-Captain and full CAD/manufacturing ownership for Typhoon and Leviathan.

I completed the CTE Engineering Pathway in high school, going through the full Titans of CNC machining program and building hands-on experience with manual mills, lathes, and CNC equipment from scratch.

At Duke I'm a member of the Aero Club. I'm interested in problems that sit at the intersection of mechanical design, manufacturing, and controls — especially in robotics and aerospace.
