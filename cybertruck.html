Tesla Cybertruck

CTE Engineering Pathway — Pacific Grove High School

Scale model of the Tesla Cybertruck machined from wood stock on a CNC mill. Required multi-sided setups to produce the angular geometry and wheel cutouts.

Process

  • CAD modeled in Fusion 360
  • CAM toolpaths generated in Fusion 360
  • Machined on Tormach 1100MX CNC mill
