Kraken & Cthulhu

FIRST Robotics Competition — Charged Up

My Role

Manufacturing Lead — first year in the role. CAM'd and machined major structural components across both robots. Built the manufacturing subteam from 2 people to 8+ capable machinists. Created and maintained the parts organization spreadsheet. Served as Drive Coach for the competition season, directing the driver and leading match strategy.

The 2023 season produced two distinct robots. Kraken was the team's first competition swerve drive robot, featuring a double-jointed arm with gas-shock suspension. Cthulhu was designed and built in two weeks between regionals as a more efficient evolution.
