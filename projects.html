2025 BreakerBots 5104

Leviathan — Reefscape

FIRST Robotics Competition

My Role

Team Co-Captain & Manufacturing/CAD Lead. Designed the elevator system and initial coral/algae manipulator. Led mechanical integration across all robot subsystems, ensuring every sub-team's components came together correctly and on schedule.

Leviathan is capable of scoring on all four levels of the reef and operating almost entirely autonomously — even during teleop. Between its 4 localization cameras, ZED stereo camera, and 5 coprocessors, it represents the team's most technically complex robot to date.

Key Systems

  • Elevator — Custom 3-stage cascade elevator extending 80" in under 1 second. Counter-balanced with constant force springs, 8 lb effective driven mass, fully 3D-printed gears.
  • Vision — Real-time SLAM on Nvidia Jetson with 4x global shutter cameras. Custom CUDA-accelerated stereo depth pipeline for 3D game piece tracking.
  • Automation — Single-button scoring on any reef node, ground intake via stereo depth, full climb automation.
  • Climb — Custom polycarbonate grappling-hook with winch and spring-loaded deployment.

Awards

  • Innovation in Control Award — Contra Costa Regional
  • Industrial Design Award — NorCal Invitational
  • Judges' Award — Pinnacles Regional
  • Gracious Professionalism — San Francisco Regional

2024 BreakerBots 5104

Typhoon — Crescendo

FIRST Robotics Competition

My Role

Team Co-Captain, CAD Lead, Manufacturing Lead. Designed the chassis, intake internals, and original amp mechanism end-to-end. Led all technical meetings and robot assembly.

Typhoon features intelligent two-button teleop controls — the robot decides what action to perform autonomously. ML-powered note tracking via Limelight 3 and multi-note autonomous routines made it one of the team's most capable competition robots.

Key Systems

  • Shooter — PIDF-controlled pivot on 9:1 reduction with 3D-printed herringbone rack and pinion. Pre-tuned firing solution for shooting from anywhere.
  • Vision — 4x Arducam OV9281 on 2x OrangePi 5 running PhotonVision. 200Hz multi-threaded Kalman odometry fusion. ML note pursuit via LL3 with Google Coral.
  • Drivetrain — SDS MK4i L3 swerve with Kraken drive motors.
  • Autonomous — 1–5 note autos with AD* replanning and AI note tracking.

Awards

  • Regional Finalists — Monterey Bay Regional

Plop — Charged Up Off-Season

FIRST Robotics Competition

My Role

Led the mechanical design of Plop. Designed the chassis, elevator, elevator carriage, and end effector manipulator from scratch. Competed at Madtown Throwdown off-season event.

A development platform for next-gen technologies, Plop uses a combined elevator/wrist system to rapidly and precisely score cones and cubes in all levels of the grid autonomously — even during teleop.

Key Systems

  • Elevator — Dual Falcon grey-T style elevator with motion magic control
  • Vision — Quad Arducam OV9281 cameras on dedicated OrangePi coprocessors running PhotonVision
  • Drivetrain — SDS MK4i L1 swerve with field-centric heading-compensated control

Kraken & Cthulhu — Charged Up

FIRST Robotics Competition

My Role

Manufacturing Lead — first year in the role. CAM'd and machined major structural components across both robots. Built the manufacturing subteam from 2 people to 8+ capable machinists.
